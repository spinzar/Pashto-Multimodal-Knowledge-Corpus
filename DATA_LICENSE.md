## Overview

The Pashto Multimodal Knowledge Corpus (PMKC) is an open research project that aggregates and organizes multimodal data for the Pashto language, including text, images, audio, video, metadata, and structured annotations.

Because the corpus contains data collected from multiple public sources, **different records may be subject to different copyright and licensing terms**.
